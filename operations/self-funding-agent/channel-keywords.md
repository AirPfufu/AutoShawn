# Channel and Keyword List

## Channel Priorities

### Priority 1: Public Buying Intent

- Freelance marketplace project posts.
- Public job posts for sales assistant, lead generation, CRM cleanup, or content operations.
- Public founder or agency posts asking for outbound, research, or automation help.

### Priority 2: High-Fit Company Lists

- Local agencies with clear B2B offers.
- AI automation consultants.
- CRM and marketing operations consultants.
- Web design, SEO, paid ads, and content studios.

### Priority 3: Inbound Content

- Short posts explaining how the service works.
- Sample prospect research teardown.
- Before-after outreach examples.
- Weekly build-in-public update without exposing private prospect data.

## Buying-Intent Search Phrases

Use these as web, marketplace, or social search queries:

- "looking for lead generation help"
- "need B2B leads"
- "hiring lead generation specialist"
- "sales assistant needed"
- "CRM cleanup freelancer"
- "HubSpot cleanup help"
- "Apollo leads help"
- "need appointment setter"
- "cold email personalization help"
- "content repurposing assistant"
- "AI automation consultant lead generation"
- "agency needs outbound sales"
- "small business sales pipeline help"
- "founder looking for sales help"
- "marketing agency lead research"

## Negative Filters

Avoid prospects that mention:

- Guaranteed sales only.
- Scraping private personal data.
- Bulk spam.
- Crypto, gambling, adult, or clearly deceptive offers.
- Requests to bypass platform or email rules.

## First Weekly Test

Run one batch per channel:

- 20 marketplace or public request leads.
- 20 job-post-derived leads.
- 20 agency/company website leads.

Score all 60 leads and only prepare outreach for Priority A and strong Priority B prospects.
