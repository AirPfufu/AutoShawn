# Lead Scorecard

Use this scorecard before adding a prospect to the active outreach queue.

## Score Fields

Each field is scored from 0 to 3.

| Field | 0 | 1 | 2 | 3 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Buyer fit | Unrelated | Possible buyer | Clear B2B operator | Direct match to target buyer |
| Pain signal | No signal | Weak inferred pain | Public signal of need | Explicit demand or hiring signal |
| Ability to pay | No budget sign | Solo or very early | Small team with paid offer | Established team or agency |
| Reachable path | No contact path | Generic form only | Public email or profile | Direct decision-maker path |
| Urgency | No urgency | Evergreen need | Recent activity | Active request or job post |
| Compliance risk | High risk | Unclear | Low risk | Public business contact and relevant context |

## Priority Bands

- 15-18: Priority A. Prepare personalized outreach.
- 11-14: Priority B. Keep for batch research or softer content touch.
- 7-10: Priority C. Save for later, do not spend manual effort yet.
- 0-6: Reject.

## Required Notes

Every qualified lead needs:

- Source URL or source description.
- Why this prospect might need the offer.
- What not to claim.
- Suggested opening angle.
- Next action.
