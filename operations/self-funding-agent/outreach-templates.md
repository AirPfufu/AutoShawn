## Compliance Rules

- Personalize every message with a real public signal.
- Keep frequency low.
- Do not imply a prior relationship.
- Do not claim client results that do not exist.
- Add opt-out language when using email workflows that require it.
- Do not contact private personal addresses gathered from non-public sources.
