# AI Lead Research Sprint

## Positioning

AI Lead Research Sprint helps small B2B teams find better-fit prospects and prepare personalized outreach without hiring a full-time sales assistant.

## Best-Fit Buyers

- B2B service firms selling to small businesses.
- AI automation consultants.
- CRM, sales operations, and marketing operations consultants.
- Web design, SEO, paid ads, and content studios.
- Local service providers testing outbound sales.

## Buyer Pain

These buyers usually know they need more sales conversations, but they do not have time to research accounts, personalize messages, or keep a clean prospect list.

## Offer Packages

### Trial Sprint

Price test: 299-499 CNY or 49-99 USD.

Deliverables:

- 30 researched prospects.
- One pain hypothesis per prospect.
- One first-touch message per prospect.
- 10 follow-up drafts.
- One summary of strongest segments found.

### Weekly Sprint

Price test: 799-1499 CNY or 149-299 USD.

Deliverables:

- 75 researched prospects.
- One pain hypothesis per prospect.
- One first-touch message per prospect.
- 25 follow-up drafts.
- Weekly segment and objection summary.

### Monthly Pipeline Support

Price test: 2999-5999 CNY or 599-1199 USD.

Deliverables:

- 300 researched prospects.
- Prioritized prospect list.
- Personalized first-touch drafts.
- Follow-up drafts.
- Weekly review of replies and objections.
- Recommended segment adjustments.

## Proof Assets Needed

- One sample prospect sheet with fictional or public example data.
- One before-after example showing generic outreach versus personalized outreach.
- One short explanation of the research method.
- One clear boundary statement: no spam, no bought private data, no fake claims.

## Delivery Boundary

The service prepares research and outreach assets. It does not guarantee sales, impersonate the client, send mass unsolicited messages, or bypass platform rules.
