# Weekly Self-Upgrade Review

## Review Cadence

Run once per week. The purpose is to decide what to scale, change, or stop.

## Questions

1. What earned money this week?
2. What created qualified sales conversations?
3. What produced activity but no buying intent?
4. What objection appeared repeatedly?
5. What should be scaled next week?
6. What should be changed next week?
7. What should be stopped next week?

## Decision Rules

| Signal | Decision |
| --- | --- |
| Paid trial or strong buying reply | Scale the same segment and channel next week. |
| Replies but no conversion | Rewrite offer and reduce the ask to a smaller test. |
| Good-fit leads but no replies | Improve personalization and test a different subject/opening. |
| Many low-fit leads | Change search queries or target segment. |
| No replies after a full test batch | Stop the channel until the offer changes. |
| Compliance risk appears | Pause the channel and redesign the workflow. |
